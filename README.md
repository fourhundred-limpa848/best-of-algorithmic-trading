## 📊 Using Technical Analysis

Technical analysis helps you read market trends. You study price history to predict future moves. The software in this repository provides indicators like Moving Averages, RSI, and MACD. 

A Moving Average smoothes out price data. It shows the trend of an asset over time. You use it to decide when to buy or sell. Relative Strength Index (RSI) measures if an asset is overbought or oversold. If the RSI is high, the price might drop soon. If it is low, the price might rise.

## 🛡️ Managing Risk

Trading holds risks. Markets move fast. Bugs in your code cause losses. Start with small amounts. Use a paper trading account if the bot offers it. Paper trading uses fake money to test your strategy. You experience how the bot behaves without risking your capital.

Never leave your bot unattended for long periods at the beginning. Watch the trade logs regularly. Check the order history to see if the bot performs as expected. Verify that the bot places orders at the correct price and time.
